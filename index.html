About One Hyde Park

The exceptional collaboration between leading architects, designers, artists, and hoteliers that One Hyde Park: The Residences at Mandarin Oriental, London, delivers ultimate perfection and a unique experience on every level. The vision of Candy & Candy has become a reality in the creation of the most exquisite interiors for the most exclusive address in the world. Each apartment has been designed to complement the beautiful and carefully considered proportions of the architects` design and every detail has been accounted for to ensure the experience of life at One Hyde Park is unsurpassed. Award-winning interior designers Candy & Candy have exclusively designed all the communal areas including the spa and recreation facilities, the grand double-height reception space, Park Library, and Serpentine Suite as well as exclusively designing the interior base build of each apartment.

Residence Features

This one-bedroom residence includes an Entrance hall/cloakroom, utility cupboard, coffee/champagne bar, reception room, dining area, kitchen, master bedroom with en-suite bathroom, powder room, state of the art security system, a large storeroom, wine cellar, underground parking space at basement level, and terrace access to One Hyde Park’s extensive facilities and amenities are available exclusively to residents.

The apartment also features the use of mirrors and reflective finishes maximizes space and adds depth plus texture, providing a modern interpretation of city living.

Residence Summary

Interior designed by Candy & Candy, with Italian marble bathroom and extensive wardrobe space, this one-bedroom residence is one of the finest of its kind in the building, overlooking the trees of the planted courtyard below and on to a city view of Knightsbridge beyond.

In addition to the highest standard of internal specification, including triple glazed windows, solid oak joinery, chilled ceilings, and a state‐of‐the‐art Building Management System, the apartment benefits from the myriad services, leisure and entertainment facilities offered exclusively to the residents of this exceptional building.

The unit also includes provisional writing for audio-visual systems and programmed lighting controls which will enable residents to select their preferred suppliers for all their personal audio-visual requirements.

Master Bedroom

The master bedroom features a king sized head board built right into the wall, with a rich soft carpet and blackout blinds, and double coatded windows to ensure silence and privacy.
